About the series

Every city guide on the internet is either a list of the same ten restaurants or a sea of paid placements. Neither is useful when you actually have a trip to plan.

These guides take a different approach: cross-reference the publications that still do real reporting – the Times, Eater, Boston Magazine, Infatuation, the local alt-weeklies – find what they agree on, then organize around how a trip actually unfolds. Tiered by consensus. Mapped by neighborhood. Annotated with what to order, when to book, and what to skip.

No affiliate links. No paid placements. Just a careful read of the field.
