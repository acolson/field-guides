Part 1 Where to stay

Hotels, by Neighborhood and Vibe.

In progress. Pittsburgh hotels will mirror the methodology used for Boston and Toronto — neighborhood-first, vibe-tagged, signal-weighted (Forbes, Hotels Above Par, Travel + Leisure, Pittsburgh Magazine).

For now, the short version: stay Downtown, in the Cultural District, or in the East End. Downtown gets you walkable arrival from the airport; the East End (Shadyside, East Liberty, Bloomfield) puts dinner on foot.

A few names worth searching while this section gets researched: Fairmont Pittsburgh (Downtown, the polished anchor), The Joinery, Curio Collection (Downtown, design-forward, Christian James restaurant inside), Hotel Monaco Pittsburgh (Cultural District, Kimpton), The Industrialist Hotel (Downtown, Autograph Collection), AC Hotel Pittsburgh Downtown (Marriott), Ace Hotel Pittsburgh (East Liberty, the East End move). The Eat section is fully built out; start there.
