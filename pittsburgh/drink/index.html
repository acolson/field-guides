Part 3 Where to drink

Bars, by Kind of Night.

In progress. Pittsburgh's bar scene runs deeper than its national reputation suggests — there's a real cocktail-room layer in the Strip and Lawrenceville, dive culture in the South Side, and a few wine rooms that punch above their weight.

For now, the short version: Lawrenceville for cocktail rooms, Strip District for the natural-wine bars, South Side for the dives, and Downtown for the hotel bars.

A few names worth searching while this section gets researched: Hidden Harbor (Squirrel Hill, the tiki room), Bar Marco (Strip, the cocktail-and-natural-wine flagship — also a Tier 1 dinner pick), The Vandal (Lawrenceville, late-night cocktail), Acacia (South Side, the speakeasy), Allegheny Wine Mixer (Lawrenceville, natural wine), Bridges & Bourbon (Downtown, whiskey program).
