Part 03 Where to drink

Bars, by what kind of night you're having.

Coming soon. The plan: organize by occasion — pre-dinner cocktail, late-night dive, wine bar that wants the whole evening, hotel bar that takes itself seriously. Less ranking, more matching.

In the meantime, a few that always come up in the research: Drink in Fort Point for cocktails (no menu, you describe what you want), Yvonne's downtown for the speakeasy treatment, haley.henry in the Financial District for wine, and The Hawthorne in Kenmore for Jackson Cannon's classic program.

A handful of the dinner picks in the Eat section also work as a bar-first stop — Saltie Girl for an oyster and a martini, Eastern Standard for a 1am old-fashioned, Toro for the bar seats without a reservation. Until this section is built out, those are reliable.
