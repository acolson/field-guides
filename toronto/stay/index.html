Part 1 Where to stay

Hotels, by Neighborhood and Vibe.

In progress. Hotels don't lend themselves to the same cross-publication consensus that restaurants do – there's no equivalent of Toronto Life's 100 Best for hotels. Working on a different framework for this section.

For now, the short version: stay downtown south of Bloor. Financial District for the train-easy walk, King West for dinner energy, Yorkville for the quieter version. From any of those, Chinatown and the Distillery District are short walks, and Little Italy and Ossington are sub-$10 Ubers.

A few names worth searching while this section gets researched: The St. Regis Toronto (Financial District, the train-arrival splurge), The Hazelton (Yorkville, classic), 1 Hotel Toronto (Yorkville, new), The Drake Hotel (Queen West, scene), Ace Hotel Toronto (Garment District, design-forward), The Anndore House (Yorkville, boutique). The Eat section is fully built out; start there.
